 🚗 Electric Vehicle Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) data to uncover insights related to vehicle types, manufacturers, trends over time, and geographical distribution.

The dashboard is designed to provide **data-driven insights** for understanding EV adoption patterns and market trends.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze total EV adoption and distribution
* Compare **Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV)** vs **Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles (PHEV)**
* Study **year-wise growth trends** in EV usage
* Identify **top vehicle manufacturers (Make-wise analysis)**
* Explore **geographical distribution of EVs**

---

## 📌 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 1. Overview Page

* Total Vehicles, Avg Range, Avg Age
* BEV vs PHEV distribution (Donut Chart)
* Trend analysis using line charts
* Interactive filters (Make slicer)

---

### 🔹 2. Deep Dive Analysis

* Make-wise vehicle distribution (Pie Chart)
* Year & Make comparison
* Tree map for EV type and brand analysis
* Detailed breakdown of manufacturers

---

### 🔹 3. Geographical Insights

* Map visualization of EV distribution
* City/County-level analysis
* EV type comparison across regions

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* BEVs dominate the EV market compared to PHEVs
* Significant growth observed after 2015
* Certain manufacturers (like Tesla) contribute major share
* EV adoption is concentrated in specific regions
